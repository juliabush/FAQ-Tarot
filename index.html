FAQs
What is tarot?
Tarot is a deck of symbolic cards used for guidance, self-reflection, and exploring inner thoughts or life situations. Each card carries its own imagery and meaning, often drawing on archetypes and emotional themes.
Is tarot accurate?
Tarot is less about predicting the future and more about offering insight. Its accuracy often depends on the clarity of the question and the openness of the person receiving the reading—it's a reflective tool, not a fixed answer.
How specific should I be in my input?
Being specific in your question helps the Tarot AI give you a more tailored and meaningful response. General questions work too, but more detail can bring out deeper and more relevant insights from the cards.
How can I learn more about tarot?
You can learn more through books, online resources, and even by practicing with a deck yourself. Understanding the symbolism of each card and trying simple spreads can quickly deepen your connection with the tarot.
